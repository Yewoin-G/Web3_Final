Concept

The project is about the integration of generative AI within extended reality (XR) to generate content from the physical environment. The app utilizes near real time images and audio transformation to visualize how people perceive the same environment differently.

The project explores the overlaying of alternate realities and our perceptions, on top of our real existing physical environment with users' input through prompts. This opens up an interplay and blending of the physical and digital world which unlocks users imagination and make that imagination into an augmented and immersive space and it also extends our own reality and enable us to see other’s reality in this world.

It specifically focuses on how people from different cultures and background are represented in public spaces. The platform allows users to see the world through other cultural perspectives
